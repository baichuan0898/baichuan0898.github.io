English Listening Resources

Englishpage.com has created this collection of listening resources to help advanced English learners learn English online. The resources below include free online news and culture broadcasts, online music, online movies, online radio and more. (Most resources work best with high-speed Internet access.)

National Public Radio

  • Talk of the Nation - Audio news talk show.
  • Morning Edition - Morning news talk show
  • All Things Considered - Audio talk show on a variety of topics
  • Motley Fool Radio - Personal finance and investing talk show
  • Car Talk - Talk show about cars and more
  • This American Life - Life and culture.
  • NPR News
  • NPR Music
  • NPR Arts and Entertainment
  • NPR Podcast Directory

BBC Online

  • BBC News Hour
  • BBC Hard Talk - Journalists debate current events.
  • BBC Americana - Current issues in America.
  • BBC The Witness - Real-life accounts of major events.
  • Radio 5 - Sports and news
  • Radio 4 - Talk, culture, literature, and news
  • Radio 4 Extra - Radio comedy, drama and programming for children
  • Radio 2 - Music and entertainment
  • Radio 1 - The latest music
